Understanding BMI: Body Mass Index

In the realm of health and fitness, Body Mass Index (BMI) serves as a fundamental tool for assessing weight relative to height.

BMI is calculated using a formula based on weight and height, providing a numeric value that categorizes individuals into different weight status classifications.

BMI Categories:

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5 to 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25 to 29.9
  • Obese: BMI 30 or greater
